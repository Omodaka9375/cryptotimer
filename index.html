What is a CryptoTimer?

It’s a process for safe, conditional transactions based on user predefined timestamps.

It enables users to secure their accounts from lockout or losing account access by predefining fallback address to transfer the funds (ETH, ERC-20 & ERC-721) after a set period of time.
